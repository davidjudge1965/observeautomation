# References — The Invoice Is Sent. Now the Real Work Begins.

Sources used in researching this article.

## General small business late payments

- [2025 UK Small Business Late Payments Report — QuickBooks UK](https://quickbooks.intuit.com/uk/blog/small-business-late-payments-report-2025/)
  62% of UK small businesses dealing with overdue invoices; average owed £21,400; average SME loses £22,000/year waiting on late payments.

- [SMEs Owed £70.4bn As Late Payment Crisis Persists — Startups.co.uk](https://startups.co.uk/news/late-payment-crisis/)
  Total outstanding late payments owed to UK SMEs collectively.

- [Late Invoice Statistics 2025 — Clockify](https://clockify.me/late-invoice-statistics)
  49.3% of invoices paid late (June 2023–June 2024); 38 businesses close every day due to overdue invoices; 50,000 closures per year attributed to late payments.

- [Small Business Owners Spending Time Chasing Late Payments — Commercial Domestic Investigations](https://www.commercialdomesticinvestigations.co.uk/small-business-owners-spending-time-chasing-late-payments/)
  86 hours per year spent chasing invoices; FSB estimate of up to £5,200/year in lost time.

- [UK Businesses Face Record Late Payments in 2025 — Coface](https://www.coface.com/news-economy-and-insights/2025-uk-payment-survey-companies-face-rising-payment-delays-amid-buyer-cash-flow-concerns/)
  90% of UK companies experienced late payments in 2025; average payment delay 32 days.

- [Late Payments Affecting Businesses in 2024 — Apollo Finance](https://www.apollofinance.biz/small-businesses-severely-impacted-by-late-payments/)
  Cash flow impact data; businesses with 30+ day overdue invoices significantly more likely to report credit and hiring difficulties.

- [Late Payments UK: Which Sectors Pay Invoices Slowest in 2025 — Apollo Finance](https://www.apollofinance.biz/late-payments-uk-which-sectors-pay-invoices-slowest-in-2025/)
  Sector-by-sector breakdown of payment delays; construction subcontractor invoices average 47 days late.

## Trades and construction specific

- [Pay up! The average tradesperson is chasing £6,000 in late payments — Direct Line Group](https://www.directlinegroup.co.uk/en/news/brand-news/2024/081020242.html)
  81% of UK tradespeople chasing late payments; average owed £6,210 across 7 outstanding invoices; 42% reporting the problem is getting worse; largest single invoice written off averages £4,390; 1 in 4 have lost payments over £2,500; 8% have written off over £10,000. Research conducted August 2024.

- [Late payments research: variations in payment performance across sectors — GOV.UK](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/late-payments-research-performance-and-practices-across-business/late-payments-research-understanding-variations-in-payment-performance-and-practices-across-business-sectors-and-sizes-html-executive-summary)
  Government research on late payment patterns across business sectors and sizes.

- [UK Construction Sector Report — Allianz Trade](https://www.allianz-trade.com/en_GB/insights/economic-research/uk-construction-sector-report.html)
  £1.2 billion in late payments across UK construction; sector running on average margins of 2–3%.
