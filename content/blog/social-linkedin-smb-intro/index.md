Most small businesses are losing hours every week to tasks that should not need a human.

Emails piling up. Invoices that never got chased. Receipts sitting in a folder waiting to be entered. None of it is complicated work. But it all takes time, and time is the one thing you do not have enough of.

Our automations take those tasks off your plate.

An email-sorting automation reads your inbox and labels messages by priority, so nothing important gets buried. An invoice receipt processor reads PDFs or emails and posts expenses straight to your accounting system, saving around ten minutes per invoice. An automated payment-chasing workflow sends escalating reminders to overdue clients every morning, without you having to think about it.

Every business is different. But the result is always the same: less time on admin, fewer things slipping through the cracks, and more capacity for the work you actually get paid for.

If there is a repetitive task in your business you would rather not be doing, it is probably automatable.
