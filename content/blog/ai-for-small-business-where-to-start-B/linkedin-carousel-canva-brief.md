# Canva Brief: AI for Small Business — LinkedIn Carousel

---

## Canvas setup

- **Slide size:** 1080 × 1080 px (square — LinkedIn carousel standard)
- **Slides:** 8
- **Font:** Poppins (available free in Canva)
- **Export:** PDF (standard quality) or PNG per slide

---

## Brand colours

| Name | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Navy dark | `#0a1628` | Dark slide backgrounds |
| Navy mid | `#142240` | Dark slide backgrounds (gradient end) |
| Primary blue | `#4facfe` | Accents, borders, highlights |
| Deep blue | `#063b9e` | Headings on light backgrounds |
| Teal | `#00c9a7` | Stats, callout numbers |
| Amber | `#ffa751` | Emphasis, warnings, contrast highlights |
| Light grey | `#f8f9fa` | Light slide backgrounds |
| Light blue-grey | `#eef3fb` | Card/box backgrounds on light slides |
| Body text | `#333333` | Body text on light backgrounds |
| White | `#ffffff` | All text on dark backgrounds |

---

## Typography scale

| Element | Font | Weight | Size (px) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cover headline | Poppins | ExtraBold (800) | 72–80 |
| Slide headline | Poppins | Bold (700) | 52–60 |
| Subheading | Poppins | SemiBold (600) | 36–40 |
| Body text | Poppins | Regular (400) | 28–32 |
| Small/caption | Poppins | Regular (400) | 22–24 |
| Stats/numbers | Poppins | ExtraBold (800) | 80–100 |
| Logo/URL | Poppins | Medium (500) | 22 |

---

## Global layout rules

- **Margins:** 60 px on all sides (safe zone for LinkedIn cropping)
- **Logo:** Bottom-right corner, every slide. Use OA_Logo_Square.jpg, ~80×80 px
- **Slide number:** Bottom-left, small (22 px), colour `#4facfe` on dark slides, `#888888` on light slides. Format: "3 / 8"
- **Progress bar (optional):** Thin 4 px bar at the very top of every slide. Fill colour `#4facfe`. Fill width scales with progress (e.g. slide 3 = 3/8 of full width)

## Canva tips

- Search "Poppins" in the font selector — it's in Canva's free library
- Use "Brand Kit" in Canva to save the hex colours above so they're available on every slide
- The progress bar can be built as a thin rectangle — duplicate it across slides and adjust width
- Align everything using Canva's alignment guides (View > Rulers and guides)
- Export as PDF (standard) for LinkedIn carousel upload — LinkedIn accepts multi-page PDFs as carousels
