## Post

Most small business owners know they miss calls. Very few have sat down and worked out what each one actually costs.

For a trades business missing five calls a day, with a 30% conversion rate and an average job value of £200, that's roughly £6,000 a month going elsewhere. Not because of anything wrong — just because one person can't do the work and answer the phone at the same time.

The new post on the website runs through the maths honestly, looks at why voicemail doesn't solve it (most callers hang up rather than leave a message), and covers the options: human receptionist, call-answering service, or AI voice receptionist.

If you're a tradesperson, florist, or service business and this sounds familiar, it's worth five minutes to do the calculation for your own business.
