## Post

Most business owners spend ten minutes every morning sorting email. On a busy day, that sorting gets rushed — and somewhere in the pile, something that mattered gets missed.

I built an automation that reads incoming emails and sorts them automatically. Supplier invoices go to one place, customer enquiries to another, newsletters get moved out of the way. The categories are set up in a spreadsheet — no coding involved. It sorts and labels; it doesn't reply to anything or take any action without you.

The running cost for a typical inbox is a few pounds a month. The time it saves is considerably more than that.

If this sounds useful, the full article explains how it works: https://www.observeautomation.com/blog/email-sorting-automation/
