## You're on a job. Your phone rings. You can't answer.

By the time you're free to call back, they've already tried two more numbers — and booked someone else.

That's not bad luck. That's how people look for tradespeople, especially when something's gone wrong. A leak. No heating. A fuse board that needs replacing. They want someone who picks up. If you don't, the next person on Google does.

The AI Voice Receptionist answers every call you can't — immediately, professionally, without interrupting the job you're on.


## The real cost of a missed call in the trades

When someone calls a florist and gets voicemail, they might try again later. When someone calls a plumber with no hot water, they won't.

Emergency and urgent enquiries are the most valuable calls you'll receive. They're also the ones most likely to go elsewhere the moment they hit voicemail.

>>- A **boiler replacement** is worth £2,000–£4,000. Miss the January enquiry and it goes to whoever picked up.
>>- A **bathroom refit** can run to £5,000–£10,000. The job goes to the first tradesperson who sounds available.
>>- **Emergency call-outs** — burst pipes, total power loss, no heating in winter — go to whoever answers first, every time.

And that's before the calls that aren't worth your time at all: lead generation companies, cold callers, tool suppliers, insurance — interrupting you mid-job for nothing.


## What the AI Voice Receptionist does

When a call comes in that you can't answer, the AI Receptionist picks up — immediately, every time, 24 hours a day.

It's trained specifically on your business, so it can:

>- Tell callers your call-out charge, areas covered, and typical availability upfront — filtering out non-starters before they waste your time
>- Take a full message with contact details, job description, and location — so you know exactly what you're calling back about
>- Book or check appointments directly in your calendar
>- Recognise genuine emergencies and transfer the call straight through to you or a colleague
>- Redirect sales calls and lead generation companies — politely but firmly — so they never interrupt you on the tools again
>- Send you a full transcript of every call the moment it ends

It speaks naturally and professionally. Callers get a helpful response. You get the information you need to call back prepared.

The AI speaks naturally and professionally. Whether to disclose it's an AI is your choice — many businesses prefer to be upfront, and callers respond well when the AI is genuinely helpful.

The AI can be configured to recognise emergency situations — callers reporting flooding, loss of power, no heating in winter — and transfer the call straight through to you or a colleague immediately.

**What about the spam calls I get constantly?**
The AI handles those too. Lead generation companies, cold callers, and sales calls are redirected to email or simply declined — professionally, without any of your time wasted.
