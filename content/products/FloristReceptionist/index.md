## Frequently asked questions

**Will callers know they're speaking to an AI?**
The AI speaks naturally and confidently. Whether to disclose it's an AI is your choice — many businesses prefer transparency, and callers generally respond well when the AI is helpful and responsive.

**What if a call is urgent or complex?**
The AI can be configured to transfer calls to you or a colleague in specific situations — for example, if a caller uses certain keywords or explicitly asks to speak to a person.

**What happens to calls outside the AI's knowledge?**
If the AI can't answer a question, it takes a message and ensures you're notified promptly. It never guesses or makes up information.

**Can I update the AI myself?**
Changes to your scripts, FAQs, and settings are handled as part of your monthly service. Just get in touch and updates are typically live within 24 hours.

**Is there a minimum contract?**
There is a minimum initial term of 3 months. This gives the system time to bed in and allows you to see the full value of the service. After that, rolling monthly.
