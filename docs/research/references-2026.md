# Research References — UK SMB AI & Automation 2026

Sources used in market research, competitor analysis, and thought leadership article drafts.

## Market Statistics & Adoption

**AI Adoption Rates in UK SMEs: 2025 Survey Insights — ProfileTree**
Key source for adoption rates: 35% of UK SMEs actively using AI in 2025, up from 25% in 2024. Also covers sectoral differences (professional services vs. construction) and the 45% figure for at least one AI tool integrated by 2024.
https://profiletree.com/ai-adoption-rates-in-uk-smes-2025-survey-insights/

**Turning Point As More SMEs Unlock AI — British Chambers of Commerce**
BCC/Intuit research confirming the 35% active AI usage figure and the shift in SME sentiment. Notes that only 33% now have no plans to adopt AI, down from 43% the previous year.
https://www.britishchambers.org.uk/news/2025/09/turning-point-as-more-smes-unlock-ai/

**Most SMEs Still Struggling to Embrace AI — British Chambers of Commerce**
Earlier BCC survey (2024) showing 43% of SMEs had no plans to use AI at that point. Useful for tracking the year-on-year shift in sentiment.
https://www.britishchambers.org.uk/news/2024/07/most-smes-still-struggling-to-embrace-ai/

**New Research Reveals SMBs with AI Adoption See Stronger Revenue Growth — Salesforce**
Salesforce survey data linking AI adoption to stronger revenue growth among SMBs, supporting the business impact case.
https://www.salesforce.com/news/stories/smbs-ai-trends-2025/

**AI Adoption Among Small Businesses Surges 41% in 2025 — Thryv**
US-skewed but relevant: documents the acceleration in small business AI adoption and which tools are being adopted most rapidly.
https://investor.thryv.com/news/news-details/2025/AI-Adoption-Among-Small-Businesses-Surges-41-in-2025-According-to-New-Survey-from-Thryv/

## Barriers & Sentiment

**Major Barriers to AI Adoption Remain for UK Businesses — techUK**
Detailed breakdown of barriers: difficulty identifying use cases (39%), lack of expertise (35%), cost (30%), uncertainty around ROI (25%). Key source for the "don't know where to start" stat.
https://www.techuk.org/resource/major-barriers-to-ai-adoption-remain-for-uk-businesses-despite-growing-demand-new-report-reveals.html

**1 in 3 UK Small Businesses Held Back by AI Fears — Onrec**
Source for the stat that 31% of UK small businesses are scared to implement AI despite acknowledging its benefits. Also covers IP concerns (1 in 4 SMEs worried about losing IP to AI).
https://www.onrec.com/news/statistics/1-in-3-uk-small-businesses-held-back-by-ai-fears

**We Polled UK SME Leaders About AI Adoption — YouGov**
YouGov survey of UK SME leaders covering attitudes, concerns, and what would encourage adoption. Useful for sentiment context.
https://yougov.com/en-gb/articles/52730-we-polled-uk-sme-leaders-about-ai-adoption-heres-what-they-said

**Henley Business School: 56% of Brits Optimistic But Overwhelmed by AI**
Source for the "FOBO" (Fear of Becoming Obsolete) research: 61% of UK workers feel overwhelmed by AI's pace, while 56% are excited about its potential. Useful for the "curious but daunted" framing.
https://www.henley.ac.uk/news/2025/henley-business-school-is-redefining-fobo-56-of-brits-are-feeling-optimistic-but-overwhelmed-when-it-comes-to-ai

**UK SMEs Tackle Growing AI Risks — Peninsula Group**
SME survey highlighting rising concerns about AI risks, including data security and IP. Context for the objections section.
https://www.peninsulagrouplimited.com/blog/uk-smes-tackle-with-growing-ai-risks-survey-highlights-rising-concerns/

## Business Impact

**AI Automation for Small Business UK: Complete 2026 Guide — Whitehat SEO**
Covers the £29,000 average annual saving and 122 hours of admin time per employee figures (citing Yell Business Report and Google UK productivity study). Key source for quantifying the ROI case.
https://whitehat-seo.co.uk/blog/ai-automation-for-small-business

**Management Practices and the Adoption of Technology and AI in UK Firms — ONS**
ONS analysis of management practices and AI/technology adoption across UK firms (2023 data, published March 2025). Provides authoritative baseline for UK-wide adoption patterns.
https://www.ons.gov.uk/economy/economicoutputandproductivity/productivitymeasures/articles/managementpracticesandtheadoptionoftechnologyandartificialintelligenceinukfirms2023/2025-03-24

**A Third of UK Businesses Plan AI Investment in 2026 — BM Magazine / Lloyds Barometer**
Lloyds Bank Business Barometer data: 82% of businesses using AI report boosted productivity, 76% report improved profitability. Also covers the FSB 14.8% revenue growth figure for innovative SMEs.
https://bmmagazine.co.uk/in-business/uk-businesses-ai-investment-2026-lloyds-barometer/
